Credits

COWX (COlorado + Weather) aggregates publicly available weather, air quality, agricultural, and aviation data for Colorado. This project is unofficial and is not affiliated with NOAA, the National Weather Service, EPA, CSU, PurpleAir, or any other data provider.

Why this site exists

I built COWX because I wanted hyper-focused, consolidated Colorado weather in one place — current conditions, forecasts, alerts, air quality, ag stations, aviation observations, and the links to dig deeper — without ads, clutter, or a dozen open tabs. Once it was useful for me, sharing it felt like the obvious next step: neighbors, pilots, farmers, firefighters, and anyone else who needs clear local weather should be able to open a page and get the detail without fighting a feed of promotions.

Weather & forecasts

  • Open-Meteo — model forecasts (current, hourly, daily)
  • National Weather Service / api.weather.gov — active alerts, Area Forecast Discussions, point-forecast links

Air quality

  • AirNow (EPA) — official AQI observations when configured
  • PurpleAir — community PM sensors when configured

Agriculture

  • CoAgMET — Colorado State University / Colorado Climate Center research stations

Hydrology & snowpack

  • USGS Water Data — real-time streamflow, gauge height, and water temperature
  • NRCS / SNOTEL — high-elevation snow depth and snow water equivalent

Aviation

  • Aviation Weather Center — METAR and TAF products

Maps & UI

  • Base map tiles: CARTO / OpenStreetMap contributors
  • Map library: Leaflet
  • CDOT / COtrip traffic cameras and RWIS: maps.cotrip.org (CARS Program camera API + CDOT ArcGIS weather stations)
  • CWOP / APRS weather packets via aprs.me
  • Radar overlay: RainViewer
  • NOAA / NWS interactive radar: radar.weather.gov
  • GOES satellite (CSU CIRA / RAMMB SLIDER): rammb-slider.cira.colostate.edu
  • Weather icons: Meteocons by Bas Milius (MIT)
  • Typeface: Outfit via Google Fonts
  • Client IP geolocation: third-party CORS geo APIs (see How it works)
